### Quick Reference — What do these terms mean?

| Term | Plain-Language Meaning |
|------|------------------------|
| **Phishing** | A fake email, message, or website designed to trick you into giving away passwords or personal details |
| **Malware** | Harmful software that can damage your device or steal your data |
| **Virus** | A type of malware that spreads from device to device |
| **Encryption** | A way of scrambling information so only the right person can read it |
| **2FA (Two-Factor Authentication)** | An extra security step (e.g., a code sent to your phone) when you log into an account |
| **HTTPS** | A secure version of a website address — look for the padlock icon 🔒 |
| **ETA 2063** | Nepal's Electronic Transactions Act — the law that covers cybercrime in Nepal |
