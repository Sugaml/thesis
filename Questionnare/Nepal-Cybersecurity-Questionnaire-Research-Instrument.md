## Section 3: Risk mitigation (Objective 3)

*Seven items (D1–D7). How often? (Never → Always)*

1. I update security or antivirus on my devices. (Nair, 2025)
2. I check for a lock sign before I enter passwords or card details. (Nithyasri, 2022)
3. I change my passwords from time to time. (Nithyasri, 2022)
4. I log out when I finish on a shared or public device. (Bognar, 2024)
5. I turn on two-factor authentication for important accounts when it is offered. (Nair, 2025; Adhikari et al., 2026)
6. I back up important files to another drive or cloud storage. (Adhikari et al., 2026)
7. I check who sent an email before I open links or attachments. (Ngamcharoen et al., 2024)
